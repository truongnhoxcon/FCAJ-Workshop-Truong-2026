> Here, I would like to share my personal feedback regarding my experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program, helping the organizing team improve potential areas based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is very friendly and open. FCAJ members are always willing to help whenever I encounter difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace is tidy and comfortable, helping me focus better.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor provides very detailed guidance, explains clearly when I don’t understand, and always encourages me to ask questions. The admin team supports administrative tasks, provides necessary documents, and creates favorable conditions for me to work effectively. I especially appreciate that the mentor allows me to research and try solving problems myself before receiving direct instructions.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks I was assigned align perfectly with the knowledge I learned at university, while also introducing me to real-world cloud service areas I had never encountered before. This allowed me to both strengthen my foundational knowledge and gain practical hands-on skills.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills such as using project management tools, writing technical architecture documents, and improving teamwork skills. The mentor also shared valuable real-world experiences that helped me better plan my future career path as a Cloud Engineer.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The work culture is very positive: everyone respects each other, works seriously but still keeps things open and enjoyable.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
Offering flexible working hours when needed.

### Answers to Survey Questions

- **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
  What I found most satisfying was the dedication of the Mentors and the enthusiastic support from the FCAJ community.
- **What do you think the program should improve for future interns?**  
  I hope the program can organize more in-depth technical sharing workshops, where mentors can dive deep into common real-world troubleshooting scenarios and cost optimization methods for large-scale deployments.
- **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**  
  I would recommend this to my friends. This is an excellent environment for students and freshers who want to push their limits, build solid self-learning habits, and develop a strong foundation in cloud computing.

- Provide additional video lectures that combine theoretical foundations with detailed hands-on lab guides for core services.
