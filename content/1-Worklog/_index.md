**On this page**, you will find the introduction to your worklog: **how** did you complete it? **How many weeks** did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do during those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Get acquainted with AWS and basic services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Learn EC2 virtual servers and storage solutions](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Learn advanced storage services and backup](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Learn identity management and IAM security](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Learn database services on AWS](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Resource interaction and Big Data processing](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Get acquainted with Docker, Amazon ECR, and containerization](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [System monitoring with CloudWatch, CloudTrail, and X-Ray](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Set up VPC Peering, Transit Gateway, and CloudFront](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Run local testing with Docker Compose and write Terraform](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Deploy services used in the project on the AWS Console](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Test and fix system errors, and write report](1.12-week12/)
